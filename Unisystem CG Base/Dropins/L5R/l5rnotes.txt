L5R Dice Roller Notes
Syntax: +roll/mods X K Y
Roll X Dice and Keep Y highest (10 sided)

Modifiers:
Explode - if they roll the number, roll another dice and add to that number.
Low - keep lowest numbers instead of highest
Emphasis - 4e only, reroll 1's

TBZ Dice Roller Notes
Syntax: +roll X:Y
Syntax: Roll Attribute dice, sux are any dice equal or less than skill (6 sided)

Modifiers:
None
